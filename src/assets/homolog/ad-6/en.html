Cases

Check out some of our success stories:

Serasa Consumidor

In nine months of Media Relations, we secured over 44,000 free media reports about the company in the Brazilian press, and organized a press conference attended by 80 professionals from all major media outlets, including every national TV broadcaster.

Hotel Launches

We were responsible for launching several hotels. Our actions included press and trade events, press trips, and marketing partnerships to raise brand awareness among end consumers, in collaboration with fashion brands, social and sports clubs, restaurants, and jewelry stores.

Destination promotion

We promoted several tourism products and destinations, such as the cities of Cunha, São Luís do Paraitinga, and Buenos Aires; the Swiss region of Interlaken; and the countries Chile, Switzerland, and Ireland. We organized promotional activities in shopping centers and clubs, photography exhibitions, events for journalists, influencers, trade, and the general public, as well as partnerships with public entities and airlines for fam tours or press trips.

Virada e Viradinha Cultural - SP

We worked on some editions of Virada Cultural and Viradinha Cultural. In addition to promoting the event in the press, securing hundreds of mentions and reports in TV and radio stations, newspapers, websites, magazines, blogs, and social networks, we also participated in organizing certain activities, such as supplier and team monitoring, and supervising assembly, disassembly, and traffic and security operations.

EF for All

Creation and execution of a third-sector project to engage former clients of the multinational education company EF, which reached 170 teachers and 2,000 students across 14 Brazilian cities in just two years. The project also secured partnerships with organizations such as Itaú Social, the Ayrton Senna Institute, the Gol de Letra Foundation, and the Government of the State of São Paulo through the Family School program.

Greening - Tourism Ireland

Partnerships with Brazilian public institutions, such as ILUME SP and the City of Rio de Janeiro, to organize the Irish Greening events in 2015, 2016, and 2017. During the events, we illuminated key landmarks in green, including the Monument to the Flags and the Borba Gato Statue in São Paulo, Christ the Redeemer in Rio de Janeiro, and the Juscelino Kubitschek Bridge in Brasília.

Co-branding

Development and execution of strategic marketing partnerships to promote our clients in venues frequented by high-net-worth individuals, such as social, golf, tennis, and yacht clubs, gyms, jewelry stores, and equestrian centers. Planning and managing events to engage members, distributing gifts, collecting mailing lists for newsletters, hiring promoters, and producing promotional materials.

Kantar Group

In 2021, we began supporting all three divisions of the multinational Kantar and significantly boosted spontaneous media coverage through networking events, press release distribution, and exclusive story placements. In that first year, over 10,500 articles were published, rising to 12,000 in 2022, and exceeding 13,000 by 2024.

Obtaining free press coverage

Leveraging strong relationships with journalists in our specialized areas—business, tourism, culture, and sports—along with organizing networking events and creating and placing exclusive stories, we generate hundreds of press articles at no cost each month, as well as hundreds of posts and Stories on Instagram.
